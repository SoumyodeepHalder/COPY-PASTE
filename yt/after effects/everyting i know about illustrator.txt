crtl + mouse wheel to zoom in or out

space bar + mouse click and drag to pan around

in the layer panel click on the plus icon to create new layer

double click on the layer to rename it

click on a layer to put shapes inside it

click on the rectange tool then click and dram diagonally to create a rectangle
press shift to make it a perfect square and press alt to make to grow from the center.

use the selection tool to select an object and move it by clicking and draging.

click on the color icon to change the color of the fill and shape.

don't take too bright colors. take colors with low saturation and brightness.
